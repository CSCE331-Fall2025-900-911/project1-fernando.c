Projects I've worked on:

SNAIC

SNAIC stands for Seamless Neural Artificial Intelligence Capturer; basically, the program is meant to take a picture and capture any objects in the picture. The goal of the app was for users to be able to find products they were looking for by taking pictures of them, where then the app would find a link to purchase the item. I was part of the AI team, working with META's Segment Anything Model, and our job was to configure the model to identify objects based on where the user clicks on the screen.
